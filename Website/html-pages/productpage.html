Leeano Suono

If you're a musician, the last thing you want is to have your instruments out of tune. That's where the Leeano Suono Kit comes in. This handy Kit helps you keep your instruments in tune, so you can focus on making music. It also goes hand in hand with our new Leeano Evo series

Leeano Rumore

Can't play at night. Well, we from the Leeano software production team are proud to present our solution for this exact problem, Leeano Rumore. Leeano Rumore makes sure that all your instruments of the Leeano Evo Series switch to Silent mode when the rest period comes around.

Leeano silenzio Evo

If you're looking for a piano that can be turned down to complete silence, the Leeano silenzio Evo is the perfect choice. With the touch of a button, you can make your piano absolutely silent. And if something goes wrong, the self-diagnosis mode will tell you immediately what the problem is and how to fix it.
