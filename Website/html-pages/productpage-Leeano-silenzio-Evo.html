Our newest and ingenious instrument, the Leeano Silenzio Evo grand piano, is a combination of our over 100 years of knowledge in making custom pianos and new technology.

The Leeano Silenzio Evo cimes with a wooden stand, and it makes for a beautiful addition to any room. The high-quality polished ebony-wood gives the piano an elegant look, while still allowing it to match most décor.

The keys of the Leeano Silenzio Evo are made out of weighted wood-keys, that are made to 100 precent imitate the feel of an all ivory keyboard. The Pedals of the Leeano Silenzio Evo are also weighted and while it's muted you won't hear any damp noises. This is all due to our felt isolation that dampens the key's impact to a degree where you won't be able to tell if you're even hitting the keys properly.

The Leeano Silenzio Evo does not only come with an intricate design that was made to fit, to match any décor, it also comes with its own self-diagnosis module and built-in Bluetooth. The self-diagnosis module will tell if your Silenzio is out of tune or damaged, and will provide according solutions. The built-in Bluetooth allows you to stream music from any of your devices. Paired with the Leeano Rumore Software kit, the Leeano Silenzio Evo will enhance your experience by having automatic chord detection and a MIDI-Recorder.
