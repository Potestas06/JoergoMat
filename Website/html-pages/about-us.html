History

In the early years of the 20th century, Lee Giano founded his carpentry in Acciano, Italy . Lee Giano's carpentry quickly became known for its high-quality, handcrafted furniture. Lee Giano himself was a highly skilled craftsman, and his furniture was prized for its beauty and durability.

In the years following World War II, Lee Giano's carpentry began to experience financial difficulties. The Italian economy was in recession, and Lee Giano's customers were increasingly unable to afford his luxury furniture. In order to keep his business afloat, Lee Giano began to produce instruments and more affordable furniture. The first instruments produced by Lee Giano's carpentry were violins and Pianos.

These instruments quickly became popular, and Lee Giano's carpentry soon began to produce a wide variety of musical instruments. Today, the company is one of the world's leading producers of musical instruments, and its products are prized for their quality and craftsmanship.

The story of Lee Giano's carpentry is a remarkable tale of success in the face of adversity. Despite economic hardships, Lee Giano was able to keep his business afloat by adapting to changing market conditions. Today, his legacy continues on through his company's commitment to quality and craftsmanship.
