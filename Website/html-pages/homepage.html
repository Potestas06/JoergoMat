Who are we?

Leeano is a company that was founded by Lee Giano in 1902. The company was founded in Acciano, Italy.

Leeano is a luxury brand that produces high quality instruments manufactured of polished ebony-wood. We, Leeano besides offering a wide range of instruments, we also offer a wide range of furniture, accessories, and software for your instruments. Leeano's mission is to enhance the experience of playing instruments by providing quality products and adequate software that help them look and feel their best.

Leeano products have been praised throughout the 1920s and 1960s for the design and feel of the pianos.

The products of our company, Leeano have also been awarded multiple MMR Dealers choice-, Red Dot Design- and Acoustic Piano of the year awards, over the multiple years we've existed.

If you're looking for a high-end luxury brand, instruments, then the product we, Leeano offer are one of the best in the market.
