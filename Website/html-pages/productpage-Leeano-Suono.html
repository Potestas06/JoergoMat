If you're a musician, the last thing you want is to have your instruments out of tune. That's where the Leeano Suono Kit comes in. This handy Kit helps you keep your instruments in tune, so you can focus on making music.

While designing our tuning hammer, we made sure that it is easy to use. We also made sure that there is a tuning lever tip that is exchangeable for every instrument, our company produces. This includes tips for a baby grand piano, a square grand piano, a grand piano, and a spinet piano, we also include exchangeable lever tips for harps. This includes tuning lever tips for lever harps, pedal harps, double strung harps and wire harps.

Before you begin, be sure to pull out your smartphone and open up your Leeano Suono application and choose the following option "tune your instrument" and follow the instructions displayed on your Smartphone. The Leeano Software will inform you which nob you have to tighten for which Key. The Leeano Software will also indicate a correct key with a short beep and will move on to another key.

While tuning, just look at the built-in LCD on the tuning hammer, it will tell how many degrees you have to turn counter- or clockwise in order to bring your beloved instrument back into tune. Your LCD will remain red if the key hasn't been correctly tuned, and will switch to green if your key is in the correct tune.

Something that is also notable is that all Owners of our Leeano Silenzio Evo Grand Pianos or Evo-series in general will receive updates on the state of the Leeano Silenzio product. Those updates will contain, how out of tune your instrument is and if there is anything wrong with the instrument in general
